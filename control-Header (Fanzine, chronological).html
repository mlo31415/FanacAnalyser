Chronological List of Fanzines on Fanac.org

Another way to appreciate fanzines is to take them in context with other publications of the time. Here, on a month by month basis, you can browse through all the fanzines on our site. There are months like September, 1943, where it seems the whole world was pubbing their ish (or at least their FAPAzine). There are months like December, 1996, where there are far fewer. We continue to strive to add more fanzines, and provide a better archive of fannish writing over the last 90 years.
